Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help golfers track their scores and veiw their improvement over time. It will allow users to record each round they play so they can compare scores and see progress and log stats like range hit for different clubs.

Audience

The intended audience is beginner to intermediate golfers especially college students and young adults who want an easy way to track progress and improve their game.

Dynamic elements

I will use JavaScript to let users add, view, and delete golf rounds on the site. Each round will be stored as an object in an array and the page will update to show the rounds and calculate stats like average and best score.

Content

Home page

For Rounds Played: Record each round you play by entering the course name, holes played, your score, and the date. The site will store your rounds and automatically calculate your best scores, averages, and totals so you can see how you’re improving over time. For Driving Range Practice: Log your practice sessions by entering the club you used and the distance of your shots. The site will track your driving range stats and show your best performances so you can track progress and see improvements.

Stats

Stats Page: Your stats page will show summaries of your rounds and practice sessions, showing your top scores, averages, and progress over time. You’ll be able to quickly view your progress and view areas to improve in your game.
